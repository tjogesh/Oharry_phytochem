Data for Skogen et al. 2022. https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0031942222001017

Analysis here: https://github.com/tjogesh/Oharry_phytochem

Files included:
1. "O harry data for tania rar corr.xlsx"
- floral scent data for Oenothera harringtonii collected in the field and greenhouse

2. "OharryFieldMorph20092012_KS_revised.xlsx"
- floral morphology data for field collectedOenothera harringtonii plants 

3. "OharryGreenhouseMorph20082009.xlsx"
- floral morphology data for greenhouse collected Oenothera harringtonii plants 

4. "phytochem_lookup.csv"
- higher level chemical classification for floral scent compounds

5. "pairwiseFst.csv"
- pairwise Fst values. Data from this paper
Skogen, K. A., Overson, R. P., Hilpman, E. T., & Fant, J. B. (2019). Hawkmoth pollination facilitates long-distance pollen dispersal and reduces isolation across a gradient of land-use change 1. Annals of the Missouri Botanical Garden, 104(3), 495-511.
https://bioone.org/journals/annals-of-the-missouri-botanical-garden/volume-104/issue-3/2019475/Hawkmoth/10.3417/2019475.short

6. "o.harry cum curve.xlsx""
- data to plot cumulative accumulation curve for floral scent in Oenothera harringtonii
